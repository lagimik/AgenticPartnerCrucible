# AI Assistant Skills

AI assistant skills are reusable instruction packages that teach an AI coding assistant how to perform domain-specific work. In `skills-for-fabric`, skills encode workload APIs, query patterns, safety rules, routing triggers, examples, and operational best practices for [[microsoft-fabric]].

## Why It Matters

Skills turn implicit expertise into repeatable operating procedures. They let a general assistant behave more like a specialized practitioner without relying only on model memory.
