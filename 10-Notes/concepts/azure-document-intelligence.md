# Azure Document Intelligence

Azure Document Intelligence provides specialized AI models trained for document parsing and extraction. It is positioned for structured documents and common templates where consistency, low latency, and proven extraction accuracy are the priority.

## Best Fit

- Standard forms and fixed templates.
- Prebuilt structured document types such as invoices, receipts, IDs, tax, and mortgage documents.
- Custom extraction with labeled samples for highly structured documents.
- On-premises or air-gapped deployment through containers.
- Scenarios where existing production Document Intelligence workloads should remain unchanged.
