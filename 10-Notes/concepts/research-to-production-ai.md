# Research to Production AI

Research to Production AI is the pattern of reducing the distance between frontier AI research and practical builder adoption.

## Pattern

The Foundry Labs source describes a repeatable arc:

1. Discover frontier capabilities.
2. Try them in an interactive playground.
3. Understand the technical approach.
4. Access code, papers, or project materials.
5. Integrate the capability into a real workflow or product.
6. Use customer stories and community feedback as adoption evidence.

## Why It Matters

This pattern turns research from a headline into a developer-accessible asset. It supports Microsoft's broader 2026 positioning around AI systems that can be tried, measured, governed, and shipped rather than only demonstrated.
