# LevelUp

LevelUp (skillupwithlevelup.com) is a Microsoft-sponsored partner skilling platform operated by TechnoFocus. It provides on-demand training, certification prep, and deal-readiness resources for Microsoft partners.

## Key Offerings

| Offering | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| Courses | On-demand, self-paced training aligned to Microsoft certifications |
| Learning Journeys | Curated multi-course paths by solution area |
| Deal-Ready Accelerators | Bridge from skilling to customer engagement |
| Certification Prep | Practice tests + free exam voucher at 80%+ |
| Office Hours | Weekly live sessions with experts |

## Solution Areas Covered

- AI Business Process
- Azure (Cloud & AI Platforms)
- Security & Compliance
- Modern Work
- Business Applications

## Relationship to Partner Skilling Hub

LevelUp complements the Microsoft Partner Skilling Hub (skilling-hub.com). The Skilling Hub is the official Microsoft platform; LevelUp is a third-party (TechnoFocus) platform sponsored by Microsoft that provides hands-on certification prep and deal-readiness content.
