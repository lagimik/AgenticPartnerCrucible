## Summary

These diagrams are intended to help organisations understand where policy evaluation occurs, how signals flow between services, and how enforcement is applied consistently. Rather than prescribing a single deployment model, the diagrams illustrate common architectural patterns used to protect sensitive data across endpoints, email, and collaboration services.
