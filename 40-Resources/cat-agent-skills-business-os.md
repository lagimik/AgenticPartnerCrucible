# CAT Agent Skills - Business OS for Copilot Studio

A practical operating system for Copilot Studio that gives agents a consistent way to clarify outcomes, choose the right approach, solve problems, make decisions, plan projects, improve processes, handle incidents, and turn repeatable work into reusable skills.

## Key Differentiator

Unlike a standard Copilot experience that relies mainly on the prompt and available context, Business OS adds a structured way of working so responses are more consistent, actionable, and easier to reuse across real business tasks.

## Capabilities

- Outcome clarification
- Approach selection
- Problem solving
- Decision making
- Project planning
- Process improvement
- Incident handling
- Reusable skill creation
