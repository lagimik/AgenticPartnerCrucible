# LevelUp Deal-Ready Accelerators

Resources on the LevelUp platform that bridge the gap between partner skilling/certification and actual customer deal engagement.

## Summary

Deal-Ready Accelerators represent the "last mile" of partner enablement on LevelUp. While Learning Journeys build broad capability and certifications validate knowledge, accelerators prepare partners for specific customer conversations and deal scenarios.

## Structure

- Filterable by solution area
- Searchable catalog
- Associated with specific Microsoft product solutions
- Includes practical deal-preparation assets

## Partner Enablement Flow

```
Microskilling → Courses → Learning Journeys → Deal-Ready Accelerators → Customer Engagement
```
