# LevelUp Learning Journeys

Curated multi-course learning paths on the LevelUp platform designed to build Microsoft partner capability in specific solution areas.

## Summary

Learning Journeys are structured skill-building paths on LevelUp (skillupwithlevelup.com), a Microsoft-sponsored platform operated by TechnoFocus. Each journey aggregates multiple courses into a coherent progression targeting a partner role or solution area.

## Observed Journey Themes

- Data Security & Compliance Expertise
- Security-First Messaging for Customer Conversations
- Deal Readiness Acceleration
- Regulatory and Industry Requirements Navigation
- Secure Adoption of Cloud & AI

## Platform Features

- Filterable by solution area and role
- On-demand access
- Connected to certification prep and exam vouchers
- Weekly live Office Hours
