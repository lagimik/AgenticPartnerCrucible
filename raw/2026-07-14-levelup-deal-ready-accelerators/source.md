# LevelUp Deal-Ready Accelerators

**Source:** https://skillupwithlevelup.com/dealreadyaccelerators
**Captured:** 2026-07-14
**Issue:** lagimik/AgenticPartnerCrucible#12

---

Deal-Ready Accelerators are a category of resources on LevelUp (skillupwithlevelup.com) designed to help Microsoft partners accelerate deal readiness. They bridge the gap between skilling/certification and actual customer engagement.

## Observed Features (from page structure)

- Filterable by solution area
- Searchable catalog
- Card-based presentation with solution badges
- Grouped by solution areas with icons
- Each accelerator has a description and associated Microsoft product logos
- Feedback mechanism for continuous improvement

## Platform Context

LevelUp is a TechnoFocus platform sponsored by Microsoft that provides:
- On-demand courses aligned to Microsoft certifications
- Hands-on practice labs and exam prep resources
- Weekly live Office Hours with experts
- Learning Journeys (curated multi-course paths)
- Deal-Ready Accelerators (bridge skilling to customer engagement)

## Significance

Deal-Ready Accelerators represent the "last mile" of partner enablement — moving from skill acquisition to customer conversation readiness. They complement Learning Journeys (broad capability building) with targeted deal preparation assets.
