LevelUp (skillupwithlevelup.com) is a Microsoft-sponsored partner skilling platform operated by TechnoFocus. Learning Journeys are curated, multi-course learning paths designed to build partner capability in specific solution areas.

## Observed Journey Themes (from page content)

- Strengthen Data Security & Compliance Expertise
- Position Security & Compliance in Customer Conversations
- Accelerate Deal Readiness with Security-First Messaging
- Navigate Regulatory and Industry Requirements
- Drive Customer Confidence and Trust
- Reduce Risk and Enable Secure Adoption of Cloud & AI
- Align Solutions with Microsoft Security & Compliance Offerings

## Platform Context

- LevelUp is a TechnoFocus platform sponsored by Microsoft
- Offers on-demand courses, certification prep, hands-on labs
- Targets Microsoft AI Cloud Partner Program (MAICPP) solution designation goals
- Includes weekly live Office Hours with experts
- Free exam voucher when completing course with 80%+ on practice test
- Journeys are filterable by solution area and role

## Solution Areas (from navigation)

- AI Business Process
- Azure (Cloud & AI Platforms)
- Security & Compliance
- Modern Work
- Business Applications
