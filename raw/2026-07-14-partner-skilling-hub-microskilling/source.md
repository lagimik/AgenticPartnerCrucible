The Microsoft Partner Skilling Hub (skilling-hub.com) is the official Microsoft Partner training platform. Microskillings are a content type within the platform — short, focused learning units designed for rapid skill acquisition.

## Platform: Microsoft Partner Skilling Hub

- Official Microsoft Partner training system
- Sponsored by Microsoft
- Access exclusive training, certifications, and resources for Microsoft Partners
- Available in 10+ languages (en, fr, es, de, pt, it, ja, ko, zh-Hans, zh-Hant)
- Features an AI-powered Skilling Agent chat assistant
- Operated by Digital Arts & Sciences (DAS)

## Microskilling Content Type

Microskillings are bite-sized learning modules within the Partner Skilling Hub. They represent the smallest atomic unit of skill-building content on the platform, distinct from full courses, learning paths, or certification programs.

## Data & Privacy Notes

- Accounts inactive for 90 consecutive days may be archived
- Learning activity can be shared with employer/organization unless opted out
- Data shared with Microsoft and authorized distributors for compliance and reporting
